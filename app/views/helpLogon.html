Information about logon

If you are seeing this page, it almost certainly means that you are trying to do something with {{env.applicationName}} application and either

  • You dont have the required permissions to do what you were trying to do or, more likely,
  • Your "session" has timed out. Sessions on the {{env.applicationName}} application last for one day if you don't logout. Either way, the answer is probably simple, logon again using the "Login with Facebook" or "Login with Google" links
